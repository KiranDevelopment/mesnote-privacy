Privacy Policy – MesNote

Last updated: March 2026

MesNote ("the app") is a note-taking application. This policy describes what data the app uses and how it is handled.

1. Data we collect and use

  • Account data: If you sign in (optional), we store your email address and account identifier to provide sync across your devices. This is processed by Firebase (Google).
  • Note content: Your notes (text, images, audio, video) are stored on your device. If you sign in, only text notes are currently synced to your account in Firebase. Media notes (images, audio, video) remain on your device. We do not use note content for advertising or analytics.
  • Camera and photos: The app can use your device’s camera and photo library to add images and videos to your notes. Media is only used inside your notes. We do not access the camera or photos for any other purpose.

2. Permissions

The app may request:

  • Camera – to take photos/videos and add them to notes.
  • Microphone – to record audio in notes.
  • Storage / media – to save and attach images, audio, and video to notes.

These are only used for the features described above.

3. Where data is stored

Data is stored on your device and, if you use sign-in, on secure servers (Firebase/Google Cloud). Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS).

4. We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal data or note content to third parties.

5. Your choices

You can stop using sync by signing out in the app. To delete your account and synced data, use the account deletion option in the app or contact us (see below).
