Privacy Policy – MesNote

Last updated: 26 March 2026

MesNote (“the app”) is a note-taking application. This policy describes what data the app uses and how it is handled.

1. Data we collect and use

  • Account data: If you sign in (optional), we store your email address and account identifier so you can use the same account on multiple devices. Authentication and cloud storage are provided by Firebase (Google).
  • Note content on your device: Your notes can include text, images, audio, and video. That content is stored locally on your device (e.g. in the app’s database). We do not use note content for advertising.
  • Cloud sync (optional, beta): If you are signed in, the app may sync a limited subset of your notes to your account in Firebase: typically note titles, tags, and text messages. Images, audio, and video are not uploaded as part of this sync and are not restored from the cloud to a new device. Sync is described as beta in the app and may change.
  • Locked notes: If you lock a note, your password is not sent in plain text to the cloud. A cryptographic hash may be stored in your account so another signed-in device can verify the same password after sync. For locked notes, titles, tags, and message text are not synced to the cloud in the same way as unlocked notes; details follow the behaviour shown in the app.
  • Camera and photos: The app can use your device’s camera and photo library to add images and videos to your notes. Media is only used inside your notes. We do not access the camera or photos for any other purpose.
  • Crash and error diagnostics: The app may send technical reports (e.g. crash logs, app version, device type) to Sentry to help fix bugs. These reports are not used for advertising.

2. Permissions

The app may request:

  • Camera – to take photos/videos and add them to notes.
  • Microphone – to record audio in notes.
  • Storage / media – to save and attach images, audio, and video to notes.
  • Notifications – if you use reminders, to show alerts at the times you choose.

These are only used for the features described above.

3. Where data is stored

Data is stored on your device and, if you use sign-in, relevant account and sync data on secure servers operated by Google (Firebase / Google Cloud). Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS where applicable.

4. We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal data or note content to third parties.

5. Your choices

You can stop using cloud sync by signing out in the app. Media that was never uploaded will remain only on your device. To delete your account and synced data, use the account deletion option in the app or contact us (see below).
